Simple steps for a lifetime of healthy smiles

1

Brush Twice Daily

Use fluoride toothpaste and brush for 2 minutes, morning and night

2

Floss Daily

Remove plaque between teeth where your toothbrush can't reach

3

Rinse with Mouthwash

4

How Often Should You Visit?

Regular dental visits are essential for maintaining optimal oral health. The frequency depends on your age, risk factors, and current oral health status.

Our team will create a personalized prevention schedule based on your individual needs. Most patients benefit from visits every six months, but some may need more frequent care.

Book Your Visit
Children (2-17 years) Every 6 months
Adults (18-64 years) Every 6 months
Seniors (65+ years) Every 4-6 months
High Risk Patients Every 3-4 months
