Most patients should visit the dentist every six months for routine checkups and cleanings. However, some patients with specific conditions may need more frequent visits. Your dentist will recommend the ideal schedule based on your individual oral health needs and risk factors.

A routine exam includes a comprehensive evaluation of your teeth, gums, and mouth. We check for cavities, gum disease, oral cancer, and other issues. Digital x-rays may be taken, and we'll assess your bite and jaw alignment. The exam is followed by a professional cleaning and personalized oral hygiene instructions.

Treatment costs vary depending on your specific needs. We provide detailed cost estimates before any treatment begins and our interactive calculator above can give you instant estimates. If you have insurance, we'll calculate your out-of-pocket costs. We also offer payment plans and financing options to make treatment affordable.

We specialize in helping anxious patients feel comfortable. We offer various comfort amenities, take breaks whenever needed, and explain everything we're doing. For patients with severe anxiety, we provide sedation options including nitrous oxide and oral sedation to ensure a relaxed, stress-free experience.
