Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers about insurance and financing

What if my insurance isn't listed? +

We work with many insurance providers beyond those listed. We also file out-of-network claims to maximize your benefits. Call us at (555) 123-4567 to verify your specific plan.

Do you require a down payment for financing? +

No! Many of our financing options offer $0 down payment. You can start your treatment immediately and pay over time with comfortable monthly payments.

Will checking financing options hurt my credit? +

Our pre-approval process uses a soft credit check that won't affect your credit score. You can check your options worry-free and only proceed if you're happy with the terms.

Can I combine insurance with financing? +

Absolutely! We'll maximize your insurance benefits first, then you can finance any remaining balance. This combination helps make even major treatments very affordable.

What's included in the membership plan? +

Our membership includes 2 cleanings, 2 exams, necessary X-rays, and 20% off all other services. There are no waiting periods, deductibles, or annual maximums. It's perfect for patients without insurance.
