Information for Parents

Everything you need to know about your child's dental care

First Visit Guide

We recommend the first visit by age 1 or within 6 months of the first tooth. The visit includes a gentle exam, cleaning, and parent education.

Insurance & Payment

We accept all major insurance plans and offer flexible payment options. CareCredit available with 0% financing for 12 months.

Preventive Care

Regular cleanings, fluoride treatments, and sealants can prevent 80% of cavities. We focus on prevention first!

Behavior Management

Our team is specially trained in child psychology and uses positive reinforcement techniques to ensure comfort.
