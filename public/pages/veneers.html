Initial Consultation & 3D Scan

Your journey begins with a comprehensive smile analysis using our advanced 3D scanning technology. We'll discuss your goals, take digital impressions, and create a treatment plan tailored to your unique needs and lifestyle.

  • ✓ Complete oral examination and health assessment
  • ✓ 3D digital smile design and virtual preview
  • ✓ Personalized treatment options and timeline
  • ✓ Financial planning and insurance verification

Digital Smile Design

Using cutting-edge software, we'll create your perfect smile digitally before any work begins. You'll see exactly how your new smile will look and can make adjustments until it's absolutely perfect for your face and personality.

  • ✓ Review detailed 3D smile mockup from multiple angles
  • ✓ Adjust shape, size, color, and proportions
  • ✓ Approve final design with confidence
  • ✓ Receive beautiful temporary preview veneers

Precision Tooth Preparation

Minimal preparation ensures your veneers look natural and last for decades. We remove only 0.5mm of enamel using advanced techniques and place beautiful temporaries so you leave with a preview of your new smile.

  • ✓ Conservative tooth preparation with minimal discomfort
  • ✓ Precision digital impressions using 3D scanning
  • ✓ Custom temporary veneers placed immediately
  • ✓ Perfect color matching and bite adjustment

Master Craftsmanship

Our master ceramists hand-craft each veneer with meticulous attention to detail. Using the finest porcelain and advanced techniques, they create veneers that are both breathtakingly beautiful and incredibly durable.

  • ✓ Individual veneer sculpting by master ceramists
  • ✓ Custom shade layering for natural translucency
  • ✓ Multiple quality control checks and refinements
  • ✓ Final polish and glazing for perfect finish

Final Placement & Reveal

The moment you've been waiting for! We carefully bond each veneer using advanced techniques, make final adjustments for perfect fit and bite, and reveal your stunning new smile. You'll leave with care instructions and a lifetime maintenance plan.

  • ✓ Precise try-in and final approval process
  • ✓ Advanced bonding technique for permanent placement
  • ✓ Final adjustments and professional polishing
  • ✓ Smile reveal, professional photos, and celebration!
