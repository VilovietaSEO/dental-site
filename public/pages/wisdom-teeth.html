Common Questions

How long does wisdom teeth removal take?

⌄
The procedure typically takes 45-60 minutes for all four wisdom teeth. Simple extractions may be quicker (20-30 minutes), while impacted teeth may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate during your consultation.

What's the recovery time?

⌄
Most patients return to normal activities in 3-4 days. Complete healing takes about 2 weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available 24/7 for any concerns during recovery.

Is wisdom teeth removal covered by insurance?

⌄
Most dental insurance plans cover 50-80% of wisdom teeth removal when medically necessary. We'll verify your coverage during the free consultation and provide a detailed cost breakdown. Payment plans are available.

What are the signs I need my wisdom teeth removed?

⌄
Common signs include: pain in the back of your mouth, swollen or tender gums, difficulty opening your mouth, bad breath or taste, crowding of other teeth, or cysts visible on X-rays. Even without symptoms, impacted wisdom teeth should be evaluated.
